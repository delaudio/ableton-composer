## Rubric Name

synth-pop

## Evaluation Dimensions

- hook and lead clarity
- bass, drums, pad, and chord separation
- groove consistency with restraint
- palette coherence for the intended style
- useful contrast between verse/chorus or equivalent sections

## Common Failure Modes

- too many always-on layers with no hierarchy
- weak hook or no memorable foreground line
- bass and chords competing in the same register
- overbusy drums that break the style restraint
- sections changing density but not function

## What Not To Over-Penalize

- minimal drum programming when the harmonic and hook material carry the identity
- simple basslines if they support the groove and tonal center
- repeated hooks if they serve the song form

## Scoring Guidance

- reward clarity, restraint, and section lift
- penalize mud, crowding, and generic role blur

## Category Suggestions

Use categories such as:
- hook
- arrangement
- groove
- palette
- section_contrast
- register_balance
- tonality
