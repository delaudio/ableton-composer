## Rubric Name

string-quartet

## Evaluation Dimensions

- idiomatic ranges for violin, viola, and cello
- independence of parts instead of excessive doubling
- inner-voice motion and voice leading
- register balance and spacing
- cello foundation and low-register function
- motivic coherence and development
- harmonic clarity and texture contrast

## Common Failure Modes

- cello written too high for too long without clear musical reason
- viola underused or reduced to permanent doubling
- upper strings moving as block chords with little independence
- piano-like vertical writing that ignores quartet texture
- too little contrast between sections or textures

## What Not To Over-Penalize

- occasional doubling for emphasis
- simple textures if they are balanced and intentional
- repetitive motifs if they support clear formal development

## Scoring Guidance

- prioritize idiomatic writing and part independence
- treat register misuse and collapsed texture as major issues
- reward convincing quartet texture even when harmony is simple

## Category Suggestions

Use categories such as:

- texture
- motivic_development
- harmony
- structure
