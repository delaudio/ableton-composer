## Rubric Name

krautrock

## Evaluation Dimensions

- motorik or pulse continuity when relevant
- texture development over long spans
- timbral identity and repetition with purpose
- interplay between rhythmic persistence and harmonic restraint
- stylistic coherence for krautrock references

## Common Failure Modes

- repetitive parts with no textural evolution
- groove that loses hypnotic continuity
- too much harmonic churn for the intended aesthetic
- anachronistic palette that breaks the reference world without intention

## What Not To Over-Penalize

- repetition itself
- static harmony when texture and momentum do the work

## Scoring Guidance

- reward sustained direction, trance-like continuity, and gradual development

## Category Suggestions

Use categories such as:
- pulse
- texture
- repetition
- arrangement
- palette
- style_coherence
