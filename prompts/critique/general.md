## Rubric Name

general

## Evaluation Dimensions

- structural coherence across sections
- clarity of roles and arrangement balance
- tonal and rhythmic consistency
- contrast between sections without losing continuity
- idiomatic use of ranges and densities where inferable

## Common Failure Modes

- loop-like material presented as a full composition without contrast
- too many or too few active roles for the density implied by the style
- weak tonal center or inconsistent harmonic direction
- flat rhythmic grid with little phrase shaping
- no clear focal point or hierarchy between supporting and foreground roles

## What Not To Over-Penalize

- simplicity, if it is intentional and coherent
- sparse arrangements, if they still establish function and direction
- short sketches, as long as you clearly identify them as sketches rather than complete forms

## Scoring Guidance

- favor usefulness over harshness
- reward coherence, control, and clear intent
- penalize structural confusion more than lack of complexity
