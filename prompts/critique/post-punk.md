## Rubric Name

post-punk

## Evaluation Dimensions

- bass prominence and rhythmic identity
- guitar/synth interplay without overcrowding
- angular or tense harmonic language where appropriate
- section contrast through texture and subtraction
- stylistic coherence for post-punk references

## Common Failure Modes

- bass not carrying enough structural weight
- guitars/synths masking each other
- groove too flat or too rock-generic
- too much sustained pad fill reducing tension
- sections lacking contrast in texture

## What Not To Over-Penalize

- sparse harmony
- repetitive bass motifs
- rawness if it supports the intended aesthetic

## Scoring Guidance

- reward tension, separation, and bass-led identity

## Category Suggestions

Use categories such as:
- bass_function
- arrangement
- texture
- groove
- palette
- style_coherence
