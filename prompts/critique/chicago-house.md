## Rubric Name

chicago-house

## Evaluation Dimensions

- four-on-the-floor groove function
- bass and chord stab interaction
- economy of melodic material
- intro/outro and mix-friendly structure
- style coherence for early house references

## Common Failure Modes

- groove too busy or not sufficiently locked
- chord stabs without enough rhythmic identity
- bassline that muddies the kick
- too much melodic ornamentation for the style
- no usable DJ-oriented structure

## What Not To Over-Penalize

- repetitive material when it serves groove and mixing
- sparse melodic content if rhythm and harmony are strong

## Scoring Guidance

- prioritize groove, function, and stylistic coherence
- penalize structural unusability and low-end conflict more than harmonic simplicity

## Category Suggestions

Use categories such as:
- groove
- low_end_balance
- arrangement
- harmony
- style_coherence
- intro_outro
